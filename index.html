TREKKING TRIPS

Kareri trek

The Kareri Lake trek, located in the Kangra district of Himachal Pradesh, takes you through lush sub-tropical pine forests. The forests are filled with chir and chilgoza pines, interspersed with broad-leaf species.

Ththaran trek

Try this 5.8-mile out-and-back trail near Dharmshala, Himachal Pradesh. Generally considered a challenging route, it takes an average of 5 h 49 min to complete. This is a popular trail for birding, hiking, and walking.

Himani chamunda trek

The Aadi Himani Chamunda Temple is the main highlight of the Aadi Himani Chamunda Trek. It is located at an altitude of about 2810 meters and is surrounded by the lofty Dhauladhar Ranges.
